<h4><strong>2014 Arif Budiman Award Malay Language – Mdm Yahida Yahya</strong></h4>
<p>
<br>Arif Budiman means ‘the learned person who contributes to society’.&nbsp;This
award celebrates teachers for their outstanding contributions in the teaching
and learning of the Malay Language. The award recipients contributed to
the teaching fraternity by participating or presenting in regional and
international conferences in addition to sharing best practices in professional
sharing sessions.
