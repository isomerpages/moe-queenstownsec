<h4><strong>2025 Most Inspiring Tamil Teachers (MITT) Award and the Lifetime Achievement Award – Mr Saratkumar Ganasekaran and Dr Vijayarani d/o Govindasamy</strong></h4>
<p>In recognition of their outstanding contributions in the teaching and
learning of the Tamil Language, we are incredibly proud that in 2025, <strong>Mr Saratkumar Ganasekaran </strong>received
the <a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/news/press-releases/20250830-four-teachers-recognised-at-most-inspiring-tamil-teachers-award-ceremony-2025" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Most Inspiring Tamil Teachers’ Award</a> for
his innovative ways to connect Queenstownians with the Tamil language,
while <strong>Dr Vijayarani Govindasamy</strong> earned the Lifetime Achievement
Award for her remarkable 50 years in education. They serve as role models
for other Tamil Language educators in nurturing our students' love for
the language and using technology creatively to enhance learning.</p>

<h4><strong>2025 Inspiring Teacher of English (Leadership) Award – Mr Perdana Pan</strong></h4>
<p>We congratulate Mr Perdana Pan for being awarded the <a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/news/speeches/20251001-minister-for-education-and-minister-in-charge-of-social-services-integration-mr-desmond-lees-congratulatory-speech-for-the-inspiring-teacher-of-english-awards-ceremony-2025-at-oldham-theatre-national-archives-of-singapore" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Inspiring Teacher of English Award</a>,
which acknowledges teachers of English Language who ignite a love for the
language, and are effective in helping their students speak and write accurately.
The Leadership Award is presented to Heads of Department or Subject Heads
who lead the development and implementation of effective English programmes
in their schools.</p>

<h4><strong>2024 Best National Institute of Education (NIE) Tamil Trainee Teacher Award – Ms Bhavana d/o Rajaram</strong></h4>
<p>
<br>We are proud to share that Ms Bhavana d/o Rajaram, who joined our school
in 2024, received the <a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/news/press-releases/20240920-six-teachers-recognised-at-most-inspiring-tamil-teachers-award-ceremony-2024" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Best NIE Tamil Trainee Teacher Award</a> during
the Most Inspiring Tamil Teachers' Award Ceremony 2024. This award ceremony
celebrates the excellent performance of Tamil Language teachers in educating
and promoting Tamil Language among their students.</p>

<h4><strong>2018 Excellence in Mathematics Teaching Award (EMTA) – Mdm Jayasolai</strong></h4>
<p>The <a href="https://ame.org.sg/2020/12/04/emta-2018-recipient-jayasolai/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Excellence in Mathematics Teaching Award</a> recognises
Singapore mathematics teachers who are passionate in mathematics teaching,
have displayed exemplary efforts in the teaching of mathematics, and are
role models to their colleagues and the mathematics teaching fraternity
in Singapore. This award has been issued by the Association of Mathematics
Educators (AME) since 2017.</p>

<h4><strong>2014 Arif Budiman Award Malay Language – Mdm Yahida Yahya</strong></h4>
<p>
<br>Arif Budiman means ‘the learned person who contributes to society’.&nbsp;
<a href="https://www.mllpc.sg/assets/upload/publications/pdf/1523581194_AGAB%202014%20Booklet.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">The award</a>celebrates teachers for their outstanding contributions in
the teaching and learning of the Malay Language. The award recipients contributed
to the teaching fraternity by participating or presenting in regional and
international conferences in addition to sharing best practices in professional
sharing sessions.
