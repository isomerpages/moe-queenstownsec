<h4><strong>2014 Arif Budiman Award Malay Language – Mdm Yahida Yahya</strong></h4>
<p>
<br>Arif Budiman means ‘the learned person who contributes to society’.&nbsp;This
award celebrates teachers for their outstanding contributions in the teaching
and learning of the Malay Language. The award recipients contributed to
the teaching fraternity by participating or presenting in regional and
international conferences in addition to sharing best practices in professional
sharing sessions.

<h4><strong>2024 Best National Institute of Education (NIE) Tamil Trainee Teacher Award – Ms Bhavana d/o Rajaram</strong></h4>
<p>We are proud to share that Ms Bhavana d/o Rajaram, who joined our school
in 2024, received the Best NIE Tamil Trainee Teacher Award during the Most
Inspiring Tamil Teachers' Award Ceremony 2024. This award ceremony celebrates
the excellent performance of Tamil Language teachers in educating and promoting
Tamil Language among their students.</p>
