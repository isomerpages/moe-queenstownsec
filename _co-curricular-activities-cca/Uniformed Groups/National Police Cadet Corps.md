<h2>Key Achivements</h2>
<p>In 2024, our NPCC unit achieved the Distinction Award significant milestones,
including our Year 3 cadet Callison Tan who clinched the 5th position in
the .38 Revolver Competition. Cadets excelled in key events like the Police
Youth Ambassador and Community Safety and Security programmes, embodying
resilience, teamwork, and active citizenry, preparing them as future leaders.</p>
