<p>The logo design is by Hu Ming Hui of 3 Integrity (2025)</p>
<p>The number "70" uses the school’s signature maroon to represent fraternity,
courage and service, echoing our motto "DARE TO SERVE". Its bold, dynamic
brushstrokes tell the story of 70 years of resilience since 1956 and reflect
our enduring spirit.</p>
<p>The "uest" is in our iconic sky blue, a colour that stands for endless
opportunities, pure purpose and potential. It speaks about our mission
since 1956: to nurture learners who seek knowledge and pursue excellence.</p>
<p>The "0" cleverly incorporates the "Q" of Queenstown, linking our 70th
anniversary to our school’s rich heritage. QUEST is the affectionate name
of QUEenstown Secondary Technical and the name of the school magazine that
chronicles our journey.</p>
<p></p>
<p>70<sup>th</sup> Quest represents 70 years of heritage, a strong sense of
belonging, learning and leadership through service among our students,
and the bridge between our past, present and future.</p>
