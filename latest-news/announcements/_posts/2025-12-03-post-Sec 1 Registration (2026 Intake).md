<p><strong>2026 Secondary 1 Registration Information</strong>
</p>
<p>Dear Parents/Guardians and Secondary 1 Students,</p>
<p>Welcome to Queenstown Secondary School (QTSS)! We are delighted to have
your child/ward join our school community. This document contains key information
about the Secondary 1 Registration process and the start of school in 2026.</p>
<p>The Secondary 1 Registration process will be conducted online, so students
are not required to report in person on Registration Day. Please complete
the Online Registration form below. You will be informed of your child's
form class by Monday, 29 December 2025, via Parents Gateway (PG).</p>
<p>We are confident that your child/ward will have an engaging and enriching
learning experience at our school over the next four years. We look forward
to working closely with you in your child's/ward's educational journey.</p>
<p>For further enquiries, please call the General Office at 6474 1055 during
office hours, or email Mdm Nur Haryani, Assistant Year Head (Lower Secondary),
at <a rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">nur_haryani_mohamed_hatta@moe.edu.sg</a>.</p>
