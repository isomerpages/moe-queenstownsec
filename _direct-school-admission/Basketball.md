<p><strong>Queenstown Basketball (Boys &amp; Girls) &nbsp;</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>At QUEST Basketball, we don’t just train athletes — we shape resilient, values-driven individuals.</strong>
<br>Our programme is more than just about scoring baskets; it’s a journey
of growth, character, and excellence — both on and off the court.</p>
<p>
<br>We believe in nurturing the whole person. Beyond drills and match strategies,
our players develop through structured strength and conditioning, leadership
opportunities, team-bonding camps, and competitive exposure. Every training
session is anchored in our core values — discipline, respect, teamwork,
and responsibility — which guide how our players train, compete, and support
one another.</p>
<p>Behind every player is a dedicated coaching team and a supportive school
environment. Our teachers and coaches work closely to ensure that each
student-athlete is supported not just in sport, but also in their academic
and personal growth. We see every player as an individual, and we are committed
to helping each one reach their potential in their own way.</p>
<p>Our girls’ basketball journey is a strong reflection of what we stand
for. Formed in 2024, the team started from the ground up — building skills,
understanding the game, and shaping a shared identity. Through consistent
effort and a commitment to improving together, they went on to achieve
2nd Runner-Up in the National School Games League 3 tournament. This milestone
is not just about the result, but what it represents — that with hard work,
discipline, and belief, progress is always possible regardless of the starting
point.</p>
<p>While we celebrate this achievement, we remain focused on the process.
We believe that strong foundations and the right values will sustain long-term
growth, and we continue to challenge our players to improve, take ownership,
and support one another as a team.</p>
<p>Whether you are an experienced player or just beginning your journey,
there is a place for you in the QUEST Basketball family. We are committed
to helping every student grow in confidence, develop resilience, and find
meaning in the challenges they overcome.</p>
<p><strong>Considering DSA?</strong>
<br>If you have a passion for basketball and a willingness to learn, we welcome
you to consider joining us through the Direct School Admission (DSA) Exercise.
We encourage students and parents to choose a school that values holistic
development and provides the support needed to grow both as athletes and
as individuals.</p>
